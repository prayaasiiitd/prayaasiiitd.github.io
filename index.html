About Prayaas

We are a group of enthusiasts trying to make a real difference in our immediate environment by spreading awareness and sensitivity among individuals and groups to the total environment and its allied problems. We are delighted to inform that the Institute has approved our initiative as the ecological club of IIIT-Delhi.

What we do

We Serve

Plantation Drives

To enrich the vision of our club, we aim for greener earth. We choose a spot around, and with the help of volunteers, we plant saplings of various kinds.

Cleanliness Drives

Once in every month, we clean the roads, parks and public places.

Blood Donation

By coordinating with various NGOs, we have been organizing various blood donation drives. Volunteers are welcome to donate for the good cause.

Cleanliness Drive

2 October 2017 | 10:00

In order to re-affirm the "Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan" commitment, Prayaas: The Eco-Club of IIITD organised a cleanliness drive on 2nd of October. It saw a huge participation from students and staff.
